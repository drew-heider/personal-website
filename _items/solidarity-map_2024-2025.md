## About This Map
Map created in QGIS. The source for this map are archival materials from the Clinton Jencks Papers, held at the University of Colorado, Boulder's Rare and Distinctive Collections. Specifically, this map makes use of strike funds published by the Western Federation of Miners from 1903-05.
